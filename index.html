Eric Fabrizi

Composer

Studied for a BMus (Hons) in Electronic Music with Composition at Guildhall School of Music and Drama — 2016 to 2020

  • Founding member, organiser and co-organiser of Guildhall Experimental Music Society (GEMSoc) — February 2019 to July 2020

GEMSoc specialises in the creation and performance of text and graphic scores. I aimed to create a low-pressure environment in which everyone could try out ideas without expectations.

  • Improviser and sound engineer at The Thinking Minds Project (TMP) — from February 2020

TMP is a contemporary music collective specialising in electro-acoustic music and cross-arts collaboration.
