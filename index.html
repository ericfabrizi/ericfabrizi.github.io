Some of my roles:


  • Founding member, organiser and co-organiser of Guildhall Experimental Music Society (GEMSoc) — February 2019 to July 2020

GEMSoc specialises in the creation and performance of text and graphic scores. I aimed to create a low-pressure environment in which everyone could try out ideas without expectations.

On the 26th of April 2020, we premiered a compilation of live performances that had taken place over the internet that weekend. I invited members of the society to put forward pieces for the concert, providing the work involved some form of collaboration. I was involved in a number of the pieces as a performer, collaborator and composer. I created a software instrument that sends MIDI to multiplayerpiano.com, a site that allows users to play a virtual keyboard instrument simultaneously:
